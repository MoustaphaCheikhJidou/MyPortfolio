About Me

MJ

Final-year engineering student at École Supérieure Polytechnique (ESP), Mauritania. Currently completing my end-of-study project at the Banque Nationale de Mauritanie — building a multi-LLM RAG chatbot integrated with WhatsApp, orchestrated via N8N, with OpenAI, Gemini and Ollama as interchangeable backends.


I also teach poverty measurement and household survey methods at ISMS (Institut Supérieur des Métiers de la Statistique), and work on development project management for programs funded by the Islamic Development Bank and African Development Bank.


My stack spans Python, Next.js, PostgreSQL + pgvector, Docker, and LaTeX — with a strong interest in applied AI, data pipelines, and systems that actually work in production. As President of the ESP Data Club, I lead projects that encourage collaboration and learning in data science.
